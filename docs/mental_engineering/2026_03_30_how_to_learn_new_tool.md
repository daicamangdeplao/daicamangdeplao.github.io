# How to learn a new tool

> Know yourself and get your hand dirty

## Define the reasonable use case (goal)

* What are the concrete outcomes?
* What are the problems that the tool will solve?

## Collect enough background information

* What is the core abstraction?
* What is the flow (input- transformation - output) of adopting the tool?

## Learn manually

* Retype the example word by word.
* Learn by heart
* Add logs and comments
* Modify inputs and observe the outputs
